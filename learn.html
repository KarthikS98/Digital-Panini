Learn Sanskrit Grammar – The Pāṇinian Way

Sanskrit grammar is rule-based, logical, and beautifully structured.

Choose a topic below to begin your learning journey. Each section is designed to help you understand the fundamental principles that make Sanskrit so systematic and elegant.

Sandhi (Word Combination)

Discover how words combine and change when they meet. Sandhi rules explain sound transformations at word boundaries.

Dhātu (Root Words)

Explore the root verbs that form the foundation of Sanskrit words. Every verb comes from a basic root form.

Pratyaya (Suffixes)

Learn how suffixes are added to roots to create meaningful words. Pratyayas modify and expand the meaning of roots.

Pratyaya (प्रत्यय)

Suffixes That Create Meaning

What is a Pratyaya?

A pratyaya (suffix) is added to a dhātu (root) or another word to create new forms and meanings. Pāṇini categorized pratyayas into Kṛt (primary - added to roots) and Taddhita (secondary - added to complete words). These suffixes transform roots into verbs, nouns, adjectives, and more.

Dhātu (धातु)

The Building Blocks of Sanskrit Verbs

What is a Dhātu?

A dhātu (root) is the irreducible core of a verb—the seed from which all verb forms grow. Pāṇini identified over 2,000 roots, each carrying a fundamental meaning. By adding suffixes and applying rules, these roots transform into complete words.
